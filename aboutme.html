About Me

My name is Amber and I am a native of good 'ole Tennessee. I grew up in Clarksville where I attended Austin Peay State Univeristy with a degree in Marketing. I've been back and forth between Clarksville and Nashville for a few years, but I can finally call Nashville home. I'm currently working for the state DOT in the IT Division as an Executive Assistant. I hope to move into a development role to join my fellow IT peeps in the land of code!
I'm a HUGE foodie! I enjoy cooking and my current endeavors include curing meats and growing my own vegtables. I also do a lot of knitting and cross-stitching while I sit around with my pug, Puggy.
